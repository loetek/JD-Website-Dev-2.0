ABOUT ME

Hello! I am Justin Wheeler but you can call me JD.

I have spent most of my life in sales either as an individual contributor or managing a team. However, I came to a point where I lost all interest and passion in that industry. At the same time my entire life I had been driven by technology and my hobbies have alway been centered around technology. So I decided to take the leap and move towards my passion and pursue software development with the assistance of Nashville Software School to become a Full Stack Developer . I can honestly say I am so happy with my choice, I am excited every day and looking forward to my next step.
